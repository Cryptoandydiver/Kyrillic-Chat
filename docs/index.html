Common questions

Do you get access to all my documents?

No. For the demo, you send 3 to 5 sample files only. The demo runs on our server and expires after 7 days. It is not a working system you can keep. For the full system, we install on your server and never see your data.

What if the AI makes a mistake or deletes something?

The AI cannot delete or modify your files. It only reads and searches. Your original documents stay untouched. We also set up automatic backups.

How do I send my sample files for the demo?

After you submit the form below, I will respond with instructions. You can email the files or share a Google Drive / Dropbox link. Do not upload files through this website.

What formats do you support?

PDF, DOCX, TXT, Markdown, and common formats. If you use Google Drive, Notion, Confluence, or SharePoint, we can connect to those too.

How long does it take to set up?

24 hours for a demo with your sample files. Full installation takes 2 to 3 days depending on document volume.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Month to month. If you cancel, the system keeps working. You just lose support and updates.

Can I just use the demo without paying?

The demo runs on our server with only 3 to 5 of your sample files and expires in 7 days. It is a preview to show you how it works. It is not a replacement for the full system. The full system on your server with all your documents only gets installed after payment.
