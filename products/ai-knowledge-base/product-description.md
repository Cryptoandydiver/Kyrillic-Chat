# AI Knowledge Base — Product Description

## What It Is
A searchable AI knowledge base for your company's documents. Upload PDFs, Word files, Notion pages, Google Docs — then ask questions in plain English and get answers with citations in 3 seconds.

## What It Solves
- Employees spend ~20% of their time searching for information
- Knowledge walks out the door when people leave
- New hires take months to ramp up
- Critical documents sit forgotten in shared drives

## How It Works
1. You give us access to your documents
2. We index them (24h turnaround)
3. Your team asks questions via CLI, web interface, or chat
4. AI answers with exact citations from your documents
