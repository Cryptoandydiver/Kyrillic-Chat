# AI Knowledge Base for Legal Firms

## What It Is
A searchable AI knowledge base for your law firm's documents. Upload PDF contracts, case files, and legal documents. Ask questions in plain English. Get answers with citations in 5 seconds.

## What It Solves
- Attorneys spend 20% of their time searching for documents
- Knowledge walks out the door when partners leave
- New associates take months to ramp up
- Critical clauses get lost in thousands of PDFs

## How It Works
1. Send 3 to 5 sample PDFs via email or Google Drive
2. We build a free demo in 24 hours (on our server, expires in 7 days)
3. Test it with your team
4. If you like it, we install on your VPS (your data stays with you)
5. We maintain and update
