# Client Qualification & Sales Script

## Step 1 — Discovery Call Structure (15 min)

### Opening (2 min)
"Hi [name], thanks for chatting. I help companies like yours turn their documents into an AI search engine — so instead of hunting through PDFs and Notion for answers, you just ask. Tell me a bit about what you're dealing with."

### Problem Discovery (5 min)
- "How many documents does your team work with?"
- "How much time do people spend looking for information?"
- "What happens when someone can't find what they need?"
- "Have you tried anything like this before?"

### Qualification Checklist
- [ ] Has 500+ documents (PDF, Notion, Google Docs, etc.)
- [ ] Team of 5+ people who search for info regularly
- [ ] Current solution: nothing, shared drives, or lost in Slack
- [ ] Budget: willing to spend $200+/mo for a solution
- [ ] Decision maker is on the call

### Pricing Pitch (3 min)
"Here's how I work:
- **Installation:** $1,500 — I index all your documents and set up the system
- **Monthly:** $200 — I maintain it, update indexes, handle questions
- **Warranty:** If you don't get 5x value in the first month, I work for free until you do"

### Close (5 min)
"Does this solve the problem you described? What would need to happen for you to say yes today?"

```

Investment: $[X] setup + $[X]/month
Guarantee: 5x value or I work for free

Next step: I'll send you a 2-min video showing how it works with sample data.

Want me to send it over?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Red Flags (Walk Away)
- "I'll just use ChatGPT for free" — not qualified
- No budget / "can you do it for $50" — not qualified
- Less than 200 documents — too small, not worth it
- Wants on-prem air-gapped for $200 — complexity doesn't match price
