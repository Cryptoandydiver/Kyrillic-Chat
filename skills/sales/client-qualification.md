# Client Qualification and Sales Script

## Discovery Call Structure (15 min)

### Opening (2 min)
"I help small law firms find any contract clause in 5 seconds instead of 20 minutes of manual search. Your data stays on your server. Tell me about how your team finds documents today."

### Problem Discovery (5 min)
- "How many contracts does your firm have?"
- "How much time do attorneys spend searching for clauses?"
- "What happens when someone cannot find what they need?"

### Qualification Checklist
- [ ] Law firm with 3 to 10 attorneys
- [ ] Has 500+ documents (PDF contracts, case files)
- [ ] No internal IT / engineer
- [ ] Confidentiality is a concern
- [ ] Budget: willing to spend $50 to $150/month

### Close (3 min)
"Here is what I propose: send me 3 to 5 PDFs. I build a demo in 24 hours. You test it with your team for 7 days. If you like it, we set up the full system on your server. If not, cost is $0."

## Red Flags (Walk Away)
- "I will just use ChatGPT" — not qualified
- No budget / "can you do it for $10" — not qualified
- Less than 200 documents — too small
- Has internal IT that can deploy AnythingLLM themselves — no need for you
